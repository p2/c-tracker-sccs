Who Can Participate?

This study is open to adults ages 18 and over who have been diagnosed with hepatitis C. It is open only to residents of Switzerland and you need to be able to read and understand German or French. Finally you will need to own a compatible iPhone.

iPhone 5S or newer have special motion sensors built in to record your daily movement without draining your battery. To help make activity information as useful as possible, you should carry the iPhone with you as much as possible, for instance in your pocket or clipped on your waist belt.

If you're interested please contact one of the SCCS Clinical Centers in Switzerland.
